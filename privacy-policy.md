# Privacy Policy – Metronome

**Last updated:** February 1, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how Metronome ("the app," "we," "our") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## What Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The only data stored by the app is:

- **Rudiment BPM progress** – Your practice tempo for each rudiment is saved locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

## How Your Data Is Used

The locally stored rudiment data is used solely to remember your practice tempo between sessions. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using the device's file system. We do not use cloud storage, external databases, or any remote servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. No data from this app is sent over the internet.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from children or any users. There is no age restriction for using this app, as no personal information is gathered.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
